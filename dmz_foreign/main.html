01Unification Observation Tower

033-682-0088 481, Geumgangsan-ro, Gimhwa-eup, Cheorwon-gun, Gangwon-do

It is located on the road for Mt. Geumgang Land Tour in the Times of South-North Reconciliation and Exchange and you may watch the beautiful Mt. Geumgang with your eyes.

02Myeongpa Beach

033-680-3356 Myeongpa-ri, Hyeonnae-myeon, Goseong-gun, Gangwon-do

The white and fine sand beach in the calm seashore is harmonized with surrounding strange rocks and bizarre stones at the northernmost of East Coast and it is in the limelight as a family resort.

03Villa of Kim Ilsung

033-680-3469 280, Hwajinpo-gil, Geojin-eup, Goseong-gun, Gangwon-do

It is a 2-story stone building which was used by Sherwood Hall, a foreigner missionary, as a chapel and by Kim Ilsung as a villa.

04Wanggok Village

033-631-2120 36-13, Wanggongmaeul-gil, Jugwang-myeo

It is designated as a traditional construction preservation district and is a traditional house village where traditional houses have been preserved because renovation of houses was impossible.

05Hwajinpo Lake

033-680-3356 Chodo-ri, Hyeonnae-myeon, Goseong-gun, Gangwon-do

White sand beach consists of monazite sand which is made of crushed shells and rocks for a long time and, when stepping on the sand, the sand sounds.
