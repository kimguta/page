Spring in Gangwon-do with fully bloomed flowers A place that awaits a journey of new life
to sprout along a beautiful road covered with colors.
Rough sea and white sandy beach. This is the true image of the East Sea.
The sea of Gangwon-do embraces them all in perfect harmony.
Blue sky, red maple leaves,
and yellow maidenhair tree leaves. The lush forests and pristine valleys change into more colorful clothes in the fall.
The snow scene of Gangwon-do is
the most spectacular in the world. When everything shivers in the cold, the most exciting day is the day
when white snow falls from the sky.
