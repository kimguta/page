How to visit

contents

This is the information on DMZ Museum where you may watch the exhibition and history of DMZ at a look.
※ You may visit the Museum after completion of entry report and security education in Entry Report Center according to the
entry procedure of the Civilian Control Line and after payment of admission fee of Unification Observatory and parking charge.

Operation Hour
March to October 09:00 ~ 18:00
November to February 09:00 ~ 17:00

Every Monday and January 1st
If Monday is a holiday, the next weekday is closing day.

Admission to the museum ends 1 hour prior
to the closing hour
Time for visit

40min ~ 1hours 30min

Admission fee

Free

Parking fee

Free

Precautions in visit of Museum
  • All the spaces of the Museum are no smoking zones (smoking area is aside of outdoor toilet).
  • You may not carry foods into exhibition rooms.
  • Entry of pet animals excluding guide dogs into exhibition rooms is prohibited.
  • You should keep silence in exhibition rooms.
  • Put our cell phone in vibration of soundless mode.
    ※ If you hear explanations of works through your cell phone, use earphones as much as possible.
