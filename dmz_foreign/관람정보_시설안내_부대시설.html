The only gift containing the meaning of DMZ You may get the only souvenir of DMZ which contains traditional beauty of Korea and symbolism of DMZ.

The rest place

Available time
09:00-18:00 / Operation time of the Museum
Contact
033-680-8553

Take a rest looking at the outdoor scenery of DMZ Museum! The rest place at the outside of Exhibition Hall F1 is equipped with outdoor tables for visitors to take a rest with simple foods and beverages.
This place supplies more than 100 individual and group visitors with a comfortable rest place.
Before and after visiting the Exhibition Hall, visitors may feel the fresh air between DMZ photo zone and outdoor observatory facility.

Octagonal Pavilion

Available time
09:00-18:00 / Operation time of the Museum
Contact
-

A place to view pure East Sea in the Civilian Control Line A place of magnificent view in DMZ Museum
The outdoor garden of the Museum connected with Ecology Lake will remain good memories in visitors and has a stone tower to wish unification and Octagonal Pavilion.
When stepping on Octagonal Pavilion, visitors may watch the wide East Sea and may also see the speakers and electronic display for broadcast propaganda to North Korea. Feel the attraction of East Sea sitting in Octagonal Pavilion escaping from the history of gloomy division.
