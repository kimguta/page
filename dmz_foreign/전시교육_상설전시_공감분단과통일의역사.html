History of sympathy, division, and unification Germany, footprint of peaceful unification

You may look at the history of division and unification of Germany which is similar with Korea. We hope that the peace sprouts in the name of South-North Unification in Korean Peninsula through the story how divided East Germany and West Germany could be unified in spite of difficult situations until peaceful unification.

The Berlin Wall between the East Germany and the West Germany was constructed by the East Germany in 1961 and was a barrier to divide West Germany and East Germany each other.
East Germany strictly prohibited move to other countries over the border and even mercilessly aimed guns at escapers of East Germany who intended to climbing over the wall for freedom. The Berlin Wall was a cold concrete wall to divide East Germany and West Germany in past. At last, the gate of the Berlin Wall was opened widely on November 9th, 1989. The Berlin Wall which was broken after peaceful unification of Germany became the symbol of peaceful unification of the Germany.
