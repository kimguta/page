History

contents

DMZ Museum will keep the values which have been kept by Korean Peninsula.

DMZ Museum took the first step in 2009 on the northernmost Civilian Control Line in eastern coast where Geumgangsan Mountain is seen.
DMZ generated by a tragic war is a historical cultural resource which attracts interest of the world and gives strong impression over Korean Peninsula.
DMZ started as unblessed remains but DMZ Museum will lead preservation and inherit the remains.
