Overview of DMZ

contents

❝ DMZ, the way we have never gone
but we have to go and are willing to go.
Stories awaken from sleep now.

DMZ where as many stories as the traces of buried days are sleeping
This place to maintain peace made me remind painful wounds sometimes
and generous breath of the Mother Nature is felt in the space ruined by the war.

Unblessed birth caused by The Korean War in past

Conclusion of Ceasefire Agreement on July 27, 1953 The war for three years and one month ended making a record,
“The longest ceasefire negotiation in the world.”

And, in the place where gunshots stopped and

in the most tragic space of Korean Peninsula,
DMZ was reborn as a symbol of peace.

DMZ, the remains of cold war which are alive and breath still

Total 1,129 days of The Korean War Destruction of 43% of industrial facilities and 50% of mine facilities in South Korea
USD 2.28 billion of total damage
621 thousand persons of South Korean army’s damage
800 thousand persons of North Korean army’s damage

Korean Peninsula stained with blood and wounds became the only divided
country in the world and we live with the deep scar of 248km across the south and north.

DMZ, the symbol of future peace we are willing to go

Registration in UNESCO as Gangwon Ecological
& Peaceful Biosphere Reserve on June 19, 2019
 Inhabitation of 6,168 kinds of wild animals
(18 species in Grade 1 and 84 species in Grade 2 Crisis of Extinction)

We should listen to the stories of DMZ
by awakening the history which has been in sleep for a long time
and by breathing into the dead land and should distribute the stories.
