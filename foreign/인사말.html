Greetings, everyone. I welcome every citizen and netizen for visiting the Taebaek website.

Taebaek mayor Ryu Tae Ho

Taebaek, located at the center of the Baekdudaegan Mountain Range, which is the backbone of the Korean Peninsula, is a historic city where our sacred Taebaeksan Mountain, Hangang River, Nakdonggang River, and Osipcheon Stream rise.

We will strive to build 'Taebaek with clean air, the center of the Baekdudaegan Mountain Range' to breathe in nature by promoting a clean city, a green city, and a human city.

I, as well as all public officials, promise to act up to expectations of those who push for changes through new consciousness.

The door of mayor's room is always open and is ready for your endless supports and participations to become the place of communication for citizens and netizens.
Thank you.
