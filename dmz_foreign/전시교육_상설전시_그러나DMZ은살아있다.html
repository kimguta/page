3 ZONE But DMZ is still alive Suspended time, hope sprouts.

DMZ is not a space stuck in the ideology of past war. It should not be forgotten that DMZ is a breathing alive space out of the suspended time. We should remember what the historical value of DMZ is for us and what the shape of reborn DMZ as a space for fetal movement of life will be. A crisis is followed by an opportunity. Now, after passing the tragic past, DMZ is alive.

The everglades ecosystem of DMZ shows how farms and low places were changed to wetlands. The place is the nests and moving routes of migratory birds including rare species such as white-naped cranes and hooded cranes and endangered wild pants and unique plants grow there. A natural ecosystem interested by the world is alive and breaths near the land that the soldiers of the south and the north of DMZ watch each other.

❝DMZ, a historical land❞

DMZ had a history before The Korean War, too. Our historical remains can be found on the remains excavated from south-north railway of Donghae Line and road sections. It is proved that DMZ has different historical meaning on the remains and videos of each region including geological properties and ancient histories, etc. of eastern areas of DMZ.

❝DMZ, a land of life❞

DMZ which was devastated by the war was reborn as a natural ecosystem in a unique shape with return of animals and plants which had left DMZ. Various animals and plants are continuing their life in the place out of human reach escaping from crisis of extinction. Especially, the wide wetlands and plains are the nests of migratory birds of Northeast Asia and are the land of life for various plants to grow on.

❝Seungil Bridge, a bridge for hope and peace❞

Seungil Bridge is the symbol of the hope for South-North Unification and the message of peace, connects Jangheung-4-ri, Dongsong-eup and Munhye-ri, Galmal-eup, Cheolwon-gun, Gangwon-do, and was designated as registered cultural property No. 26 on May 31, 2002. The bridge, of which one half was completed by North Korea and one half was completed by South Korea, is similar with DMZ, a land of peace, which stepped 2km towards the south and the north, respectively. We hope that DMZ becomes a bridge to connect the south and the north rather than disconnecting them, like Seungil Bridge.

❝River flowing in DMZ❞

Flowing water cannot be blocked. The river flowing in DMZ cannot be blocked, too. Nature has no disconnection. Birds return to their nests flying in the sky and animals climb mountains. Plants may go anywhere in the wind. We hope that DMZ becomes a river to connect the south and the north in a near future.
